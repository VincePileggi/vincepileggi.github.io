# Evidence-Based Engineering Website Blueprint

**Author:** Vince Pileggi, Ph.D., P.Eng.

---

# Vision

Develop a professional knowledge base dedicated to evidence-based environmental engineering, applied statistics, and reproducible technical reporting.

The website will emphasize engineering methods, statistical thinking, transparency, reproducibility, and lifelong learning.

Its purpose is to educate engineers, students, researchers, and technical professionals through publicly available, non-confidential resources.

---

# Guiding Principles

## Teach methods, not projects.

The website presents generalized engineering methods rather than project-specific assessments.

It is intended to communicate principles, workflows, and reproducible approaches that are broadly applicable across environmental engineering.

---

# Confidentiality

This website is intended for educational and professional development purposes.

It presents generalized engineering methods, statistical techniques, and reproducible workflows.

Any examples are illustrative and do not represent specific facilities, organizations, clients, suppliers, manufacturers, technology proponents, or regulatory assessments.

Content shall never include:

- confidential reports
- internal Ministry assessments
- project-specific operational data
- facility names
- municipalities
- suppliers
- proponents
- proprietary information

---

# Audience

The primary audience includes:

- environmental engineers
- civil engineers
- wastewater professionals
- researchers
- graduate students
- undergraduate students
- applied statisticians
- technical decision makers
